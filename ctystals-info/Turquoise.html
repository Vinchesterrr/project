What Is A Turquoise Crystal?

The root of the name ‘turquoise’ has its roots in old French. Translated it means ‘Turkish’ and the general consensus is that this is a nod to its rich history in Persia. The turquoise gemstone is recognised internationally as a traditional 11th anniversary gift while also being one of the three December birthstones.
